Biography

Dr. Lei Yang is a Research Fellow in Underground Engineering at the University of Sydney, specializing in extreme mechanics for underground engineering applications. With a strong background in geotechnical engineering and computational mechanics, I focus on developing innovative solutions for complex underground infrastructure challenges.

My research interests include rock mechanics, numerical modeling, underground construction, and the application of advanced computational methods to solve real-world engineering problems. I collaborate with industry partners and academic institutions worldwide to advance the field of underground engineering.
